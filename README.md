## 🧠 My Engineering Philosophy

If you look at my contribution graph, you won't necessarily see a wall of daily green squares. That's because **I optimize for deep work and architectural integrity over micro-commits.** I prefer to whiteboard complex architectures, build out complete, cohesive features locally, thoroughly load-test them (usually with k6), and then push the functional system. I care about writing clean, idempotent APIs and eliminating race conditions at the database level.
